Delete Your PearlMeter Account

Published by Whitemountain

Delete in the app

If you can sign in, open PearlMeter, go to Profile, and select Delete account. Confirming this request deletes your PearlMeter account, saved cosmetic scan records, and saved scan photos.

Request deletion without signing in

If you cannot access the app, email whitemountain619@gmail.com from the email address associated with your PearlMeter account. Use the subject line PearlMeter Account Deletion Request.

We may ask for information needed to verify that the request is from the account owner before deleting the account and associated data.

What deletion does and does not remove

Account deletion removes the PearlMeter account, saved scan records, and saved scan photos. Limited copies may remain temporarily in service-provider backups under their retention processes.

Deleting a PearlMeter account does not cancel an active Google Play subscription. To avoid future charges, cancel the subscription through Google Play before or after requesting account deletion.

See the PearlMeter Privacy Policy for more information about data handling.
