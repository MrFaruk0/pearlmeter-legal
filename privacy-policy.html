PearlMeter Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

PearlMeter is published by Whitemountain ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal data when you use the PearlMeter mobile application.

Information we handle

  • Account information: your email address and authentication session information when you sign in.
  • Scan content: photos that you choose to save with a PearlMeter scan, the date of the scan, and cosmetic visual-comparison measurements generated from the scan.
  • Subscription information: subscription status and purchase-related identifiers supplied by Google Play and our subscription service. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.
  • Support communications: information you provide when you contact us.

How we use information

We use this information to:

  • provide and secure your account and cosmetic tracking timeline;
  • save and display your scan history and visual comparisons;
  • provide subscription access and respond to support requests;
  • comply with legal obligations and protect against misuse.

Service providers and sharing

We use Supabase to provide account authentication, database, and private cloud-storage services. We use Google Play and RevenueCat to provide subscription purchase and entitlement services. These providers process data only as needed to provide their services. We do not sell your personal information.

Retention and deletion

We retain account information, saved scans, and saved scan photos while your account is active. You can request deletion from the account area in the app. This deletes your account, saved scan records, and saved scan photos. Limited copies may remain temporarily in service-provider backups under their retention processes.

Security

We use authenticated access controls designed to limit access to your account and saved content. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You may contact us to ask questions about your personal data or to request assistance with your account. You may also delete your account through the app as described above.

Cosmetic tracking only

PearlMeter is intended for cosmetic visual tracking and comparison. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or dental-health advice.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when our data practices or legal obligations change. We will publish the updated version on this page and revise its effective date.
